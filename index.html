About · contact

Lukas is an artist working between kinetic sculpture, interactive installation and audiovisual systems. His work makes perceptual and cognitive structures perceptible — building situations where attention, presence and pattern-recognition become material.

Trained first in psychology (B.Sc.) and interaction design (M.Sc.), he now studies in the Design, Art & Technology programme at ArtEZ, Arnhem (NL). His practice runs on hand-wound coils, microcontrollers, projection, tensioned membranes and whatever the flea market offers.

Based in Arnhem, Netherlands.
